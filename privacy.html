Privacy Policy for Sauntera

For the mobile app Sauntera by Sauntera Technologies Inc.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Sauntera Technologies Inc. ("Sauntera," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use the Sauntera mobile application (the "App") and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By using Sauntera, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

For questions, contact: support@sauntera.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information collected automatically, and information from third parties.

A. Information You Provide

When creating an account, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Mobile phone number
  • Profile photo
  • Birthdate
  • Selected preferences
  • City or location selection

If you sign in via Apple or Google, we receive the information those services provide to us.

If you contact support, we collect the information included in your message.

B. Location Information

If you grant permission, we collect your device’s precise or approximate location to provide localized recommendations.

For example, we use your location to:

  • Suggest nearby date ideas, restaurants, and experiences
  • Detect your city automatically
  • Expand recommendations to nearby cities if you enable radius settings

You can disable location access in your device settings at any time. Core functionality may be limited without location access.

C. Device and Usage Data

We automatically collect certain technical data, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App usage activity
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

This information helps us improve performance, security, and user experience.

D. Subscription and Payment Information

Subscriptions are processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

We do not collect or store credit card information.

We may receive confirmation of purchase, subscription status, and renewal dates from the platform provider.

E. Photos and Uploaded Content

If you upload profile photos or other images:

  • Images are stored securely in cloud storage
  • Deleted accounts are scheduled for deletion within 28 days

You retain ownership of your content.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

  • Provide and operate the Services
  • Personalize date idea recommendations
  • Process and manage subscriptions
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

4. How We Share Information

We may share information only as necessary with:

A. Service Providers

Cloud hosting, analytics, and infrastructure providers that help operate the App.

B. App Store Platforms

Apple and Google for subscription processing and account authentication.

C. Legal Requirements

If required by law, court order, or government authority.

D. Business Transfers

In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

We require service providers to maintain confidentiality and data protection standards.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide Services or comply with legal obligations.

When you delete your account:

  • Personal data is scheduled for deletion
  • Backup systems may retain limited data temporarily
  • Certain legal or financial records may be retained as required by law

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including Canada and the EU), you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Request data portability

To exercise your rights, contact: support@sauntera.com

7. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data.

However, no electronic transmission or storage system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

8. Age Restriction

Sauntera is intended for individuals aged 18 or older.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 18. If discovered, such data will be deleted.

9. International Data Transfers

Sauntera operates in Canada and may use service providers in the United States or other jurisdictions.

By using the Services, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data outside your country of residence.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

Material changes will be communicated via:

  • In-app notification
  • Email (if applicable)

The latest version will always be available at: https://sauntera.com/privacy
