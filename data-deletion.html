Sauntera – Data Deletion Instructions

If you signed in to the Sauntera app using Facebook and wish to request deletion of your account and associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to support@sauntera.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Facebook Data Deletion Request"
  3. In the message body, include:
    • Your full name
    • Your email address (used to sign in)
    • Optional: your Facebook User ID

Once we receive your request, we will delete your account data from our systems within 7 business days and confirm the deletion via email.

For any questions, contact us at support@sauntera.com.
